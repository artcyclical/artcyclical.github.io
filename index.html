Art is not fixed.
It is cyclical.

Every piece in our collection begins as a forgotten form. We transform these objects into unique functional furniture. Over time, the same themes and styles resurface, fall away, and reappear with renewed energy.

Each piece ships with a signed Certificate of Authenticity and a detailed provenance document tracing its journey from salvage to sculpture.
